Romanized: Ábad

Meaning: (pp.) Noun, a little bleeding, or a slight wound. Active verb and its infinitive, manabad, to cause a slight wound. P.1. the instrument; P.2. on whom. Past tense, inabaran; panabad, his knife. Also, Idiomatically, Aiabad ko ita, I can perceive/penetrate, can get the meaning though it may be difficult or obscure.
