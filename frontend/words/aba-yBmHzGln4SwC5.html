Romanized: Ábâ

Meaning: (pp. g, and not a.) Noun, delay, prolongation of time. Active verb, kumábá, mangaba, to prolong, to lengthen. P.1. That which is delayed, like, time, wages, repentance. & Verb of Mi, to become delayed. Miaban aldao, delayed/put off for many days. Active Mi, Miiabaiaba, keep on delaying, Pangaiaba, the delay. Also Kaba, length. Mi, neutral Kakaba, Quinaba, kumaba, to lengthen, to grow long by itself. Verb of Ma, mababa, meba, maba, to become long. P.l. Ikakaba &, that which grows long, or is made long. Verb: Mangaba. P.1. Ipangaba & The action, Pamangaba; If there is no case of transition, it is like Pangakaba, or, Pangaba &. The passive prolongation. Adjective, Maba, makaba, mangaba, mangaba ia bitis, He has the legs of a heron/His legs are long like those of a heron. Manga, here does not indicate plurality, add this Manga to art. 1 of chap. 9, no. 3, if not to art. 2.
