Romanized: Ábad

Meaning: (pp.) Defective root, e miabad, unceasing. Also, if I complain that your servant has not paid me back what I lent/loaned him, you will answer, Abadmo ia kasi? You should not have trusted that person, he is too astute/cunning. Also, if you come in haste and I say: you are a ray of agility, the servant answers: Abadmoko kasing tubudtuburan? There no one you could send on an errand other than myself, only a fool could be treated this way.
