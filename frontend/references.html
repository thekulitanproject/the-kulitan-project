Academic Integrity

The Kulitan Project aims to honor and preserve the contributions of generations of Kapampangan scholars, writers, historians, language advocates, and community members. This project does not claim ownership of the language, script, or cultural knowledge it documents and instead aims to contribute to their preservation, accessibility, and continued use.
